Dreamlog

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Dreamlog is a personal dream journaling app. You share things here that you might not share anywhere else — fragments of your inner life, sometimes strange, sometimes tender. We take that seriously. This policy explains plainly what we collect, why, and what we don't do with it.

What we collect

Your account. When you create an account, we store your email address and a securely hashed password. No name, no phone number, no profile.

Your dreams. The text you write or dictate is stored on our servers so you can access it across sessions. This includes dream content, titles, mood tags, and the AI-generated reflections your entries produce.

Voice recordings. If you use voice entry, the audio is recorded on your device, transcribed, then uploaded to secure cloud storage. The audio file is retained so you can replay it; you can delete it at any time by deleting the dream.

We do not use third-party analytics. We don't track how you move through the app or sell behavioural data.

How we use it

To run the app. Your dream content is sent to an AI model (Anthropic Claude) to generate titles, reflections, recurring-theme labels, and periodic insights. Voice audio is sent to a transcription service (Deepgram) to produce text. These processors receive only what is needed for each request and do not use your data to train their public models.

To keep your journal safe. Everything is stored in a private database tied to your account. Row-level security means only you can read or change your dreams.

We do not use your dreams for advertising, profiling, or resale. We do not train our own models on your content.

Who we share with

Infrastructure providers. We use Supabase (database, auth, and file storage), Anthropic (dream analysis), and Deepgram (speech-to-text). Each acts as a processor under their own terms and privacy policies.

We will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may disclose data only if required by law or to protect the security of the service.

Your choices

You can sign out at any time. You can delete individual dreams from the app, which removes their stored text, audio, and associated analysis from your account.

To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the address below and we will process the request within 30 days.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. When we do, we will change the date at the top. Continued use of Dreamlog after an update means you accept the revised policy.
